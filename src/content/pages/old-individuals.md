## Are you performing, or just getting through?

When your personal values and your workplace culture don’t align, you’re forced to "mask" - hiding your true self to avoid friction. This isn't just "part of the job"; it’s the primary cause of professional burnout. We help you identify:

- **The Energy Drains:** Which tasks leave you feeling empty by 3 PM?
    
- **The Dealbreakers:** What environments make you want to resign before you even start?
    
- **The Alignment Gap:** Where your instincts get overridden by what the company needs from you - and you stop recognising which voice is yours.
