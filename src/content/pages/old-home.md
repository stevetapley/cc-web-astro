## Four things your profile tells you

In just 20 carefully designed questions, Career Clarity builds a profile that's specific to how you actually function - not just a personality archetype.

#### 1\. Career Summary

A clear snapshot of your **career fingerprint** — the patterns, motivations and themes that shape how you work best.

#### 2\. Key Drivers

The **4–6 factors that most strongly influence your motivation at work**.

These drivers explain why certain roles feel energising while others quietly drain you.

Understanding them helps you make **better career decisions going forward.**

#### 3\. Strategic Recommendations

Practical actions you can take immediately to improve your work experience.

This might include:

• small changes in how you structure your work

• conversations worth having with your manager

• ways to align your role with what motivates you

#### 4\. Critical Focus Areas

A deeper analysis of the areas **you identified as most important.** Each focus area includes:

**Why it matters -** The underlying reason this issue affects your energy and motivation.

**Steps to take -** Practical actions you can start using immediately.

**Questions to ask yourself -** Prompts to help clarify what you really want.

**Questions to ask your manager -** Conversation starters to improve alignment at work.
